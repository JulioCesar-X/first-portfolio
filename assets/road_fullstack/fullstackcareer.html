This is the way

In the first year of college I took two Programming classes, the topic was Python, First time I had contact with a programming language. At first I was scared because I had no idea why the famous “Hello, WORLD” appeared on the screen, but somehow there was an old friend I have an affinity with, Function! I immediately realized that mathematical language would help me understand the Python language, as it is entirely made up of symbols. , has syntax rules, variables and functions. As every mathematical function “receives” a value, logically “modifies” it and “expresses” a result, I started changing the words to “input” (function that receives the data) and “print” (function that prints the data) , I just had to develop the part I like the most! Create strategies on how to process data and solve problems proposed by the teacher. I fell in love with the pace of studying, every day was a new challenge, so why not work on it? This is how I added the path of becoming a FullStack Programmer to my path, which I present to you now.
