why...a teacher?

After reading this book, by the teacher I didn't know personally but still managed to understand his way of seeing the world, I understood what a teacher is. Many fall into the illusion that the teacher teaches something, WRONG, they just show that you are also capable of knowing what he knows! They guide you along “simpler” paths because they have already traveled several complex paths, and make no mistake, they are also students like you and me. Richard Feynman showed me in this book how valuable it is to share what you learn and how it is part of learning. I had teachers who changed my life and I want to follow this path in the future, changing lives.
