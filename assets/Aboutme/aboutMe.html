Who am I?

My name is Julio Cesar Pereira, I am currently 26 years old, I was born in Paratinga, Bahia, Brazil, where I have lived 20 years of many experiences, I have met several Brazilian states and people who have helped me grow. The beginning of my life was not easy, I was adopted at birth and lost my parents very early, but I believe that for a child it was the easiest phase to go through because everything is new , as in the book "O mundo de sofia" by Jostein Gaarden, "When we grow up we fall from the peak of the rabbit's fur and lose the charm for the world". Today I've been living in Portugal for almost 6 years, I've gone through more than 13 underemployed jobs trying to reconcile my college studies, I defeated by tiredness, but life always opens new doors for me and now I'm on my way to becoming a fullstack programmer..

I'm passionate about boxing and have been practicing since I arrived in Portugal. In addition to my love for the sport, I'm strongly inspired by Muhammad Ali, who for me is one of the black heroes of the past who did much more than just make money.

I can't forget my passion for cartoons, anime and manga. For me, Rick and Morty is the best cartoon for adults.

I'm a poor astronomer, but I love stargazing. I learned a lot about astronomy from the books of Carl Sagan, creator of the 80s TV show Cosmos.
